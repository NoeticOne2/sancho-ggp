Welcome to Sancho

Sancho is a General Game Player, brought to you by Steve Draper and Andrew Rose, the creators of Quixote and Sgian Dubh.

Sancho is open source and hosted on GitHub.

  • If you want to beat us, fork the project and add your secret source!
  • If you want to join us, get in touch.
  • If you want to follow our progress, check out our blog.
